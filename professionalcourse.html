Artifact 2 Teaching with Technology Philosophy: Description: This is my philosophy of teaching using technology. I have changed my philosophy of teaching over the last 10 years. I continue to believe that students should be lifelong learners, but now I have to design lessons using technology. This will help them love learning new things so they keep growing as people. Impact: Rewriting my teaching philosophy made me think about how education has changed over the past 10 years. I am more mindful of the technology that I use in the classroom. It has to enhance the lesson in some way and needs to be an integral part of the learning process. I have learned to use the TPACK model to help me design meaningful lessons using technology. Intent: I will continue to follow the TPACK method of designing lessons throughout the rest of my career. I will start with the math curriculum, add the technology and end with the pedagogy knowledge when designing lessons for math and science. I will start with the technology knowledge, add the pedagogy and finish with the content knowledge when designing lessons for language arts and history.
