Photoshop images

I used Photoshop CC 19.1.6 Release for Windows 10 on my HP Envy Laptop to manipulate these images. I am creating the graphics that will appear in my ePortfolio final project. I started with free images from www.upslpash.com. I first used the quick selection tool to select the coffee mugs and part of the table. I saved my selection. I added another layer and copied the selection to that layer. I then used the eyedropper tool to choose a color from another image that will be on my home page for the background tint. I applied the tint to the background. Then I cropped the image using the elipse cropping tool. I wanted a circle so I held down the shift key to get a perfect circle cropped it and then sized the image at 200 x 200 pixels. I saved it as a.psd and then a .jpg.

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Open image and unlock background layer
  2. Use selection tool, make selection, save selection
  3. Go back to selection, copy it, make a new layer, paste selection
  4. Click on foreground color picker, pick color, copy hexidecimal color number, click OK
  5. Go Back to image, create empty layer, go to edit, fill
  6. Click on Contentes, color, case, put hexidecimal number in , click OK
  7. Opacity, I started at 75%, it was too dark, so I then picked 50%
  8. Use the elipse tool to select image, use shift key to make perfect circle.
  9. Copy background layer, make a new empty layer, copy the selection, paste on empty layer
  10. Save your image as a .psd. Then you can size your image and save as .jpg.
