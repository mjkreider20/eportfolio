Audacity Audio

I used Audacity to create an introduction for my ePortfolio. I downloaded two free audio clips from www.bensound.com. I recorded my voice and then imported all 3 sections of audio in Audacity. I selected 4 seconds of the beginning music and duplicated it to a new track. I used the fade out tool for the beginning music. Then I selected all of the voice audio and used the time shift tool to shift the voice to start at 3 seconds. I selected the last 12 seconds of the ending music and duplicated it to a new track. I used the fade in and fade out tool for that selection. Exported the file as an mp3 and saved it.
